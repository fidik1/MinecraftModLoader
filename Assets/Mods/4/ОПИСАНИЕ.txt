Pocket Mythology mod

Pocket Mythology mod will add various mythical creatures to MCPE, as well as a large number of new weapons and armor to the game world.

With the Pocket Mythology mod you will be able to add to the game ogres, cyclopes, goblins and other fictional creatures from the mythology of different countries. Thanks to the new mobs, the world of MCPE will become richer and more diverse, and your adventures will be even more interesting.

In addition, here you will find many new weapons, among which will be various swords and maces, as well as, new armor for your character.

New creatures from Pocket Mythology mod:

- Ents

New armor from the Pocket Mythology mod:

- Ogre Chieftain's Mask
- Ice Armor Set

New weapons in MCPE from the Pocket Mythology mod:
